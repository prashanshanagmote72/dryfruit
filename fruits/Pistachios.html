Pistachios (Pista)

Description

Pistachios are very nutritious, with a 1-ounce (28-gram) serving of about 49 pistachios containing the following :
Calories: 159
Carbs: 8 grams
Fiber: 3 grams
Protein: 6 grams
Fat: 13 grams (90% are unsaturated fats)
Potassium: 6% of the Reference Daily Intake (RDI)
Phosphorus: 11% of the RDI
Vitamin B6: 28% of the RDI
Thiamine: 21% of the RDI
Copper: 41% of the RDI
Manganese: 15% of the RDI
 Notably, pistachios are one of the most vitamin B6-rich foods around.Vitamin B6 is important for several bodily functions, including blood sugar regulation and the formation of hemoglobin, a molecule that carries oxygen in red blood cells.Pistachios are also rich in potassium, with one ounce containing more potassium than half of a large banana.
